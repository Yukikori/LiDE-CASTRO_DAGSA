Player, welcome to the Chronicle!

Mobile gaming has evolved over the years, and through this portal, you will be able to rewind time to see how it has changed and adapted with players over the years, from beloved classics such as Angry Birds and Bejeweled, to the modern flagships such as Call of Duty: Mobile and Genshin Impact.

This chronicle will give you a deeper look at how mobile gaming started, with the release of the App Store in 2008 and the first mobile games on iPhone, to present-day notable titles popular with streamers and casual mobile gamers alike.

We hope that through this chronicle, you, Player, gain a much deeper appreciation for the work that goes through creating concepts for, developing, and publishing mobile games that you know very well and love very much.
