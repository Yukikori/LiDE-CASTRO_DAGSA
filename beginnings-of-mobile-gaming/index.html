Beginnings of Mobile Gaming

The classic bird-against-pig game

Angry Birds is a casual video game wherein the player must launch birds from a slingshot in order to defeat green pigs stationed in or around structures. Birds can have special abilities, such as Bomb, who can explode upon landing, and Chuck, who can dash forward when pressing the trigger while it is in the air.
