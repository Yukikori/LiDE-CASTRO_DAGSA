Fast-paced first person shooter

Call of Duty: Mobile is a mobile first-person shooter game developed by Activision, TiMi Studios and Tencent Games, released in Southeast Asia by Garena.

Based on the famed Call of Duty franchise created by Activision, the game brings to mobile players the thrill of first-person shooting and battle royale, in the setting of Call of Duty. There are three types of games to play, namely multiplayer games, which can be played in ranked mode aside from normal mode, battle royale games, which is always played in ranked mode, and zombie games, where your mission is to ward off all zombies during the night and remain alive.

The CODM community is very vibrant, with many people playing from around the world, as veterans of the COD franchise or even as first-time COD players. With its eye-catching graphics, customizable loadouts, and fast gameplay, the game truly is one of the best in the mobile gaming scene today.
